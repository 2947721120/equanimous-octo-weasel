Sponsors

jsDelivr has been around since June of 2012 and is getting more popular every month. We currently serve around 10TB of traffic per month and we are always looking for companies that want to help us.

What we are looking for

  • CDN Companies that can sponsor their services.
    • Custom CNAME and SSL for that custom CNAME are required.
  • VPS and Dedicated servers in "exotic" locations. Currently Russia, Brazil and South Africa are the top priority.
    • Min 5GB HDD, 1TB Bandwidth, 512MB RAM, 2 cores CPU and CentOS x64 required.
  • Big dedicated servers to crunch access logs.
  • Big websites to add our RUM testing code. The more tests we get the better the performance we provide!
  • Companies and projects that are interested in using jsDelivr as their official CDN.
  • Promotion - bloggers and authors who are willing to write blog posts about our services or even IT stuff for our blog.
  • Coders/Designers to help with the website and our other projects.
  • Active people to get involved in various ways in the project.

What can we offer

  • Logo/link on our website.
  • POP location on the Map with name of the company.
  • Link in Media Mentions.
  • We will consider any other request.
