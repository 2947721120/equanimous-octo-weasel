About jsDelivr

Our Network

What makes our network unique is that it combines the powers of MaxCDN, Cloudflare and several other providers. Thanks to Cedexis, all traffic is load balanced based on availability and performance for each individual user. This unique system not only gives us the best possible uptime and performance, but also allows us to expand dynamically and add new providers on the fly. Checkout CDNperf for performance and uptime statistics.
